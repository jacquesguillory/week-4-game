Crystal Shyps

by Glen Tennis



You have crash landed on a planet unknown. Calibrate your crystals correctly to fix the engine to your crystal shyp

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. Each one has a different value, and values are rerandomized every round. When you click a crystal, it adds energy to the ship. You gotta add the crystal energy if you want off this planet.

You win by matching your total score to the given random number. You lose if your total score goes above the given number

Each time the game resets, the values of each crystal will be rerandomized.
